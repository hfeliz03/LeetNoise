LeetNoise

Save up to 6 YouTube videos and auto-play your default when the LeetCode timer starts.

First play tip

Your browser may block autoplay in a background YouTube tab until you visit it once. If the sound tab opens but does not start, just quickly switch to it and go back to your LeetCode grind.

Easiest flow: keep LeetCode on the last tab, start the music, then press Ctrl+Tab and Ctrl+Shift+Tab.

Saved videos

Pick one default. Maximum 6.

    Good video guidelines

    • Prefer 1 to 10 hour uploads so they do not end mid-problem.
    • Look for no talking, minimal intros, and stable ambient audio.
    • Brown noise is a strong default if outside noise is the main problem.
